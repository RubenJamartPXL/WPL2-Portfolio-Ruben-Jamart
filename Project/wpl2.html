Project Overview

For workplace learning 2, we tackled our biggest project yet: designing and developing a new website and a mobile app for Comfortmeubel, a furniture store in Antwerp.

Development Sprints

  • Sprint 1: Initial research.
  • Sprint 2: Creation of wireframes for the website and app.
  • Sprint 3: Visual design and website development progress.
  • Sprint 4: Finalizing the website, designing campaigns and a brand book.

Check out the revamped website here: Comfortmeubel revamped

Contributions

My responsibilities ranged from conducting extensive research, creating wireframes, ensuring functionality across the website, fixing bugs, to developing the entire backend.

Reflection

Using the STARRT-method, here is a summary of my experience and contributions:

Situation
Tasked with a complete overhaul of Comfortmeubel's website and branding.
Task
My role was to implement the design and ensure backend functionality to enhance the website's performance.
Action
Started with research, followed by wireframing, then developing a visual design and incorporating feedback into the implementation.
Result
Despite minor bugs, the website functioned well, reflecting the limitations of time and small amount of developers.
Reflection
